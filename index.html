2026 Season Standings

Raw cumulative points across all bashos played. Players who missed a basho show "—" for that tournament.

Power Rankings

Normalized ratings that account for missed bashos. The Power Rating is based on average points per basho, adjusted by a participation factor. Missing tournaments costs you, but not as much as a bad result would.
Formula: (Avg pts per basho) × (0.7 + 0.3 × participation rate) × league-difficulty adjustment

The Race — Cumulative Points

Points accumulate across bashos. Players who miss a basho stay flat while others climb.
